About FixCity AI

FixCity AI is an advanced civic engagement platform designed to empower citizens and city officials to collaboratively track, report, and resolve infrastructure issues. Combining smart data visualization, real-time status tracking, and AI-powered chatbot assistance, FixCity AI creates a transparent, efficient, and responsive urban management experience.

Our Mission

To leverage technology and civic engagement to build smarter, more sustainable cities.

Key Features

  • Smart Issue Reporting
  • Real-Time Dashboards
  • Interactive Community Features
  • AI-Powered Assistant
  • Dark/Light Mode
